C3Pred - CPP Activity Predictor

Please choose the format of the protein, of which you want to predict its activity:

From Sequence

Our tool supports protein sequences in FASTA Format, which is a simple string of the one letter characters for each amino acid. Please make sure to only use sequences containing the characters of the 20 standard amino acids

UniProtKB Accession Number

Proteins can also be submitted to the predictor by their unique UniProtKB accession number (e.g. P31946). Our tool acquires automatically the amino acid sequence and makes the prediction. For this function, an internet connection is required.

Registry ID

iGEM registries ID can also be used as an input. If the submitted ID corresponds to the nucleic acid sequence of a protein, the sequence is translated to the corresponding protein sequence and a prediction is made on the protein sequence. For this function, an internet connection is required.
